The Middle East Retail Forum (MRF) is your crystal ball into the future of retail in the MENA region. As IMAGES Group’s flagship retail storytelling platform, MRF is where the retail revolutionaries converge under one roof to forecast trends, unveil innovations, and set the agenda for tomorrow.

Join us, as MRF returns with its 13th edition and experience #ThePowerOfNext.

From Next Gen trailblazers to strategies for the future, MRF is all about what’s coming up and what’s coming next. Discover what’s relevant, what’s revolutionary, and what it takes to thrive in the dynamic world of MENA’s retail.

GLOBAL PERSPECTIVE
The 14th IMAGES RetailME Awards embody #ThePowerOfNext. We are set to recognise and honour the retailers who are shaping the future of retail in the Middle East with exceptional customer experiences across all channels, leveraging best practices and cutting-edge innovations throughout the retail value chain.
The 2024 entries for the IMAGES RetailME Awards are now open.
If your retail company or brand has achieved remarkable performance and spearheaded groundbreaking initiatives over the past year, nominate your brand now. Being recognised at the IMAGES RetailME Awards means standing out among industry peers, earning the highest honour and acknowledgement for pioneering excellence.

POWERFUL PARTNERSHIPS

Promote your products and service offering to make your brand top of mind
Get quality face-time with 10+ hours of dedicated networking
Source intelligence to help your clients
Become a provider of choice in the Retail sector
Demonstrate your thought leadership to key decision-makers

RETAILME AWARDS

CELEBRATING EXCELLENCE

The IMAGES RetailME Awards are more than just about winning in a competitive retail market like the Middle East. Serving as a motivational platform, the awards will recognise Retailers/ Technology partners that can demonstrate delivery of outstanding customer experiences to customers across all channels via best practices in tech through the retail value chain. The 2023 entries for Images RetailME Awards are now open. If your retail company or brand attained some creditworthy performance numbers and/or raised the bar with outstanding innovation and pioneering initiatives during the past year, nominate your brand now to be counted among the region’s retail stars.
